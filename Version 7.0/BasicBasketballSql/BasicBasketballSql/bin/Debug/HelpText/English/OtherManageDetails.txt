If you click on this option you can see this details:

My team games -> will show you all your team league games.

My home games -> will show you all your team games as home team.

My away games -> will show you all your team games as away team.

All league players -> will show you all the players in the league.

All league teams -> will show you all the teams in the league.

All league games -> will show you all the games in the league.
